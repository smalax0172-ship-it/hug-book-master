# Illustration Workflow

Step 1
Read STORY_CANON.md

Step 2
Identify the page being illustrated.

Step 3
Review PAGE_MAP.md.

Step 4
Review PAGE_CHECKLIST.md.

Step 5
Review image assignments:

- CHILD_Page17.png
- MOM_Page17.png
- ROOM_Page17.png
- LAMP_Page17.png
- LAMP_Page15.png

Step 6
Generate illustration prompt.

Step 7
Verify prompt against visual canon.

Step 8
Verify prompt against story canon.

Step 9
Generate image.

Step 10
Compare image to canon references.

Step 11
Reject image if canon conflicts exist.

Rules:

- STORY_CANON.md is highest text authority.
- Visual reference images are highest visual authority.
- Never redesign characters.
- Never redesign room layout.
- Never redesign lamp appearance.
- Maintain consistent art style across all pages.
